## What is Cafelua OS?
Cafelua OS is a personal AI operating system where Alpha lives in your desktop. It goes beyond chat by combining execution, automation, and system-level interaction.

## How do credits work?
Credits are deducted based on token usage and model pricing per request. The FREE plan includes a signup bonus and monthly baseline refill.

## Which AI providers are available?
The service is currently Gemini-first. OpenAI (ChatGPT) and other providers may be added later.

## How do I install the desktop app?
Sign in on lab.cafelua.com, issue a connection key, and enter it in Cafelua OS. The same account flow supports both the desktop client and bootable distribution.

## Is my data safe?
Sensitive credentials are handled server-side. The portal focuses on authentication, billing, and usage management. See our policy pages for details.
